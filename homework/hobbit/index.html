Brief Introduction of the Hobbit

Bilbo, a hobbit-lover of easy living, was enjoying himself in his hobbit-hole at bag's bottom. In the early morning, the arrival of the wise wizard Gandalf broke the peace. "Across the cold, foggy mountains, the caves that are deep underground have been built for thousands of years...". Chanting the old ballads, the thirteen dwarves dragged Bilbo into the adventure. In this "unexpected journey", the aloof hobbit Bilbo, in the dark night of the cave at the bottom of the mountain found a small ring which can change the whole world.
